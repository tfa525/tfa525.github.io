RUR was the Blueprint of the Future

This project is acreative exploration of the automated technologies of the 20th C. until now. Generative AI is a technological wonder and fraught with so many issues. Microsoft Co-Pilot was given the following prompt by the artist:

"write epic poem about technology. Start with the Rossols Universal Robots as ahistorical starting point. Describe te advancement fo technology to this date. Make sure to include personal computing, the WWW, open source software, and open source hardware. Reveal the poem was witten by generative AI."

The machine wrote a six part poem that is woven through out the project. You may find the text and other messages in tooltips, text, and links. Enter "The Dream of Silicon and Steel"
