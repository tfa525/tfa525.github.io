Distortion as Memory

Distortion feels like memory. Over time, details get blurry and small parts start to change, similar to how an image becomes pixelated after being compressed too many times. The original moment is still present, but it no longer appears exactly the same.

What may seem like a mistake in a digital image, such as glitching or low resolution, can actually create meaning. The imperfections reveal the image’s history and transformation. Instead of representing failure, distortion becomes part of the expression.
